About

My Story

I'm a UI designer skilled at empathetic design that puts the user at the centre of the process. I had a career in acting where empathy is the core value; it's essential to view things from multiple perspectives and explore divergent possibilities. I transitioned to UI Design to utilise my background in creative thinking; I'm constantly asking what other ways a user may view or interpret a product and aim to offer the most efficient, and pleasurable experience whilst helping them achieve their goals.

My approach to design is to treat every project as a living, breathing thing, that has its own personality and way of interacting with the world; as such, I aim to remain intuitive and agile in my work. I utilise my excellent communication and public speaking skills, alongside my ability to produce clear layout, effective hierarchy and logical user flows, to ensure that the user and client feel heard, understood and have the utmost confidence in the end product.

I am particularly interested in companies and projects with a strong environmental ethos, that value communication and contributing positively to the community and planet. I want to work with companies that allow me to make professional and personal discoveries, and build authentic, professional relationships.

I'm available for freelance and contract positions in the UK and remote work globally. I love to explore new places and cultures, and strive to keep learning and challenging myself. I have been learning French over the past year, and like to unwind with either an early morning run or an evening dram of whisky alongside a good film and some knitting.
